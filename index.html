SafeDM — Check messages fast, safely, and privately

Paste a message from DMs or chats and see whether it contains harassment, threats, sexual language, or manipulative patterns. Built for women and girls who want a quick, private check.

Quick Awareness

Harassment can be subtle. Messages that seem flattering can also be manipulative. Use the scanner to verify patterns and get simple next steps.

How it works

We use a keyword & pattern detector to flag risky messages. The scanner gives a color-coded result and recommended actions. It works locally in your browser — no uploads, no data sent anywhere.
