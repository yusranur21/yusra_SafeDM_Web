Help & Resources

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first.

Quick Tips

  • • Do not respond to abusive messages.
  • • Screenshot and save evidence (timestamps, usernames).
  • • Use block and report features in the app/platform.
  • • Tell someone you trust and seek local support services.

Organizations & Online Help

  • • Local women's support centers / hotlines
  • • Platform reporting pages (Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp)
  • • Legal support services (if needed)
