About SafeDM

SafeDM is a simple tool designed to help women and girls identify risky or abusive messages quickly. The prototype uses keyword and pattern detection to highlight potential risks and suggests short, practical actions.

Design & Values

SafeDM is built with empathy, dignity, and safety in mind. The language used is supportive and action-focused — not judgmental. This tool is for awareness, not for legal advice.

Color Legend

The scanner uses four colors to communicate risk:

Green — Safe: No obvious harmful patterns detected.
Yellow — Caution: May contain manipulative or suggestive language. Consider not responding and keeping evidence.
Red — Harmful: Contains harassment, sexual pressure, or threats. Consider blocking, reporting, and keeping evidence.
Black — Extremely Harmful: Explicit threats, grooming, or severe abuse. Prioritize safety — block, report, and get help.
